Fluxus is the annual TECHNO-CULTURAL festival of IIT Indore, An exceptional confluence of exquisite cultural displays with technological innovations reverberating in an aura filled with excitement, amusement and splendor. After the overwhelming success of Fluxus 2013, IIT Indore is back with Fluxus 2014 which promises to transcend all the thresholds of science and entertainment.

Fluxus 2014 would boast of unique and exciting events and performances. Pro-Shows would be the Chief crowd-pullers attracting youth from all over the country. Our stream of technical competitions sees some of the smartest minds of this country competing for supremacy.
